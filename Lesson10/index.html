Color Psychology

Blue

Blue is a primary color across all models of colour space. It often symbolizes serenity, stability, inspiration,wisdom or health. It can be a calming color, and symbolize reliability.

Serenity

Blue calls to mind feelings of calmness or serenity. It is often described as peaceful, tranquil, secure, and orderly.

Health

It lowers the heart rate. In addition, blue helps alleviate tension, stress, and problems with the immune system. It is believed to relieve insomnia, anxiety, high blood pressure, migraines, and skin irritation.

Reliability

Blue is often seen as a sign of stability and reliability. Businesses that want to project an image of security often utilize blue in their advertising and marketing efforts.
